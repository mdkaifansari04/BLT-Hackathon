About BLT Hackathons

Welcome to the BLT (Bug Logging Tool) Hackathon platform! We host regular hackathons to encourage contributions to our open source projects and build a stronger community.

Each hackathon features:

  • Real-time leaderboards tracking contributions
  • Multiple repositories to contribute to
  • Prizes and recognition for top contributors
  • A welcoming community of developers

Pick a hackathon above to get started, or check out our GitHub organization to learn more about our projects.
